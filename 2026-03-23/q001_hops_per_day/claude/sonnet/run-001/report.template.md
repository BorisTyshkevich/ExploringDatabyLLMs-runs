## Analytical Takeaway

The results reveal which aircraft and flight numbers are used for the most aggressive multi-stop rotations in a single day. High hop counts on a single flight number indicate a hub-and-spoke or sequential point-to-point routing strategy where one tail number operates continuously across many legs under one marketed flight identifier — a pattern most common among low-cost carriers optimizing aircraft utilization.
